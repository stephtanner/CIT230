Kiev, Ukraine Temple

One temple that we are located near is the Kiev, Ukraine Temple. This beautiful temple is situated on a hill to the south west of the city in the Svyatoshyns'kyi District. The Kiev temple was the 134th operating temple. It was also the first temple located within the former Soviet Union. This temple serves members from 3 stakes and 16 districts across Russia, Ukraine, Armenia, Belarus, Moldova, and Romania.

Preston, England Temple

The Preston, England Temple was a special one for President Gordon B. Hinkley. This is where the Prophet served his mission in his youth. It is located north of Liverpool and Manchester. It is part of an extensive church complex alongside the England Missionary Training Center, a distribution center, and a family history center.

San Antonio, Texas Temple

The San Antonio Temple is a beautiful temple with unique stained glass windows. The stained glass on the front of the temple rises from a dark blue to a light blue background, depicting the moon, stars, and sun. One of the stained glass windows on the back of the temple showcases the tree of life with all white fruit. This temple was the fourth temple built in Texas.
