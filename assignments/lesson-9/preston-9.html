Fresh Raspberry Ice Cream

by Stephanie Tanner

One of my favorite times of year here in Idaho is July, when raspberry season peaks. I spend most mornings meticulously picking little red berries, making sure to not miss any hiding below each leaf. I take my bounty inside to carefully wash and wait for the evening. The family gathers. First in the kitchen to prepare, then moves outside to enjoy. We grab bowls, spoons, a carton of vanilla ice cream, and our fresh raspberries. Outside we sit in the cool air after sunset and enjoy a bowl full of vanilla ice cream topped with raspberries from the garden.
