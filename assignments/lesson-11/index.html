Welcome to Simply Frigid!

Simply Frigid is your very own weather site, especially dedicated to everyone who lives in Preston, Soda Springs, and Fish Haven, Idaho. We love our small towns in Idaho and we are proud to live in these beautiful places.

Here at Simply Frigid, we are dedicated to bringing you up-to-date weather forecasts in addition to valuable information and articles about your specific towns. Be sure to check back regularly to get updates on the events that are going on in your town. We will let you know when there is a special festival, holiday, or event in your town.

We hope that you feel a part of our community. Be sure to contact us if you would like us to post anything about an event your are a part of or if you have any additional ideas for this site.
